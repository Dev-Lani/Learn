About Us

Welcome to ArithmeKit, where we're all about making math more accessible, especially regarding tricky stuff like inverse, exponential, and logarithmic functions. Our web app is here to help students like you tackle these math challenges simply!

What We Do?

ArithmeKit exists to help you understand and solve inverse, exponential, and logarithmic functions. We've compiled easy-to-use tools and video tutorials so that you can get the hang of these math concepts without the stress.
